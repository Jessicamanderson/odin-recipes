Protein Peanut Butter Cups

Description

We love a yummy protein dessert! only 162 calories for one serving!

Ingredients

  • 2/3 Cup Dark Chocolate Baking Chips
  • 1/3 Cup of PB Fit Peanut Butter
  • 1/4 Cup Maple Syrup
  • 1 tsp Vanilla Extract
  • 1/4 Cup Vanilla Protein Powder

Steps

  1. Line your 12 cup muffin tin with 12 silicone liners
  2. Empty chosen amount of chocolate chips into container and place into the microwave for 45 seconds
  3. Line each muffin tin with 1 TBS of melted chocolate
  4. Pop them in the freezer for about 10 minutes
  5. For the filling you'll add:
    • 1/3 cup of your chosen peanut butter
    • 1/4 cup of maple syrup
    • 1 Tsp of vanilla extract
    • 1/4 cup of your favorite vanilla protein powder
  6. Mix until you reach a protein ball consitency
  7. Add 1 tbsp into the muffin tins on top of the frozen chocolate
  8. Press into an even layer
  9. Finish with some flaky salt
  10. Pop into the freezer for 45 minutes and you're good to gOoooo! smiley face

  • 1/3 Cup of PB Fit Peanut Butter = 480/12 = 40 calories
  • 1/4 Cup Maple Syrup = 220/12 = 18 calories
  • 1 tsp Vanilla Extract = 12/12 = 1 calorie
  • 1/4 Cup Vanilla Protein Powder = 105/12 = 8.75 calories
  • TOTAL = 130 caloiries
