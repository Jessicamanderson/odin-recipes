Momofuku Cucumber Salad

Description

Also known as a Chilli Crunch salad! A satiating low calorie salad that'll keep you full!

Ingredients

  • Baby Cucumbers
  • A bag of Mini Sweet Bell Peppers
  • Ginger Dressing
  • Everything but the Bagel Sesame Seasoning
  • Chilli Crunch Oil - preferably Momofuku but Trader Joe's also has their own version!

Steps

  1. You'll measure with your heart for this recipe
  2. Chop as many baby cucumbers as your heart desires!
  3. Exact same - finely chop the mini bell peppers
  4. A dallop of the ginger dressing - I don't like too much but hey, again, measure with your heart! Or, if you're counting calories, a scale
  5. A bunch of the chilli crunch sauce! A spoonful or whatever
  6. Top it with Everything but the bagel mixing
  7. Mix, Admire, CRUNCH! YUMMMMM!

Calorie Count

So low it's not even worth counting!
