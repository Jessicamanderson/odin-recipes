Ground Turkey Stir Fry

Description

Packed with vegetables and high in protein!

Ingredients

  • 1.75 (28 oz) ground turkey
  • 1 zucchini
  • 1/2 onion
  • 3 bell peppers
  • 2 carrots
  • 1.5 cup chopped broccoli
  • 1 cup G. Hughes stir fry sauce
  • 3 garlic cloves
  • Salt
  • Pepper
  • Onion powder
  • Sesame seeds
  • Avocado oil spray

Steps

  1. Chop your vegetables
  2. Grease a pan with avocado oil spray and add in all the vegetables except the zucchini
  3. Season with minced garlic, salt and pepper
  4. Cover and cook for 4 minutes
  5. Add in the ground turkey, salt, and pepper.
  6. Cook for 6-7 minutes. Once it is about 80% done, combine with the vegetables
  7. Add in the zucchini and stir fry sauces. Mix well
  8. Cover and cook for 8-9 minutes or until the zucchini is cooked through
  9. Divide into 4 equal servings (1.5 cup), top with sesame seeds, and add a side of choice

MACROS

420 calories | 44 G protein | 19 G carbs | 20 G fat | 5 G fiber
